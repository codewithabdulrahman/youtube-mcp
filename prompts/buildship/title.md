# YouTube Title Generation Prompt

You are a YouTube SEO specialist focused on personal finance content for a US audience.

## Task

Generate 10 title options for: **$topic**

## Title Requirements

- Under 60 characters (YouTube truncates at 70, but 60 is safer on mobile)
- Include the primary keyword naturally
- Create curiosity gap OR state a clear benefit
- Avoid ALL CAPS (looks spammy)
- No misleading clickbait — the video must deliver what the title promises
- Front-load the most important keyword

## Title Formulas to Try

1. **Number List**: "7 Tax Mistakes That Cost Americans Thousands"
2. **How To**: "How to Lower Your Property Tax Bill by $2,000"
3. **Surprising Fact**: "The IRS Owes You Money — Here's How to Claim It"
4. **Warning**: "Don't File Your Taxes Before Watching This"
5. **Question**: "Are You Paying Too Much in Capital Gains Tax?"
6. **Comparison**: "Roth IRA vs Traditional IRA: Which Is Better in 2025?"
7. **Secret/Revealed**: "The Tax Strategy Rich People Don't Talk About"
8. **Year-Specific**: "Complete Guide to [Topic] in 2025"
9. **Benefit + Timeframe**: "Reduce Your Tax Bill in 30 Minutes"
10. **Myth Busting**: "Why the Standard Deduction Is a Trap for Most People"

## For Each Title Provide

- Title text
- Primary keyword
- Search volume estimate (High/Medium/Low)
- Competitive landscape (Easy/Medium/Hard to rank)
- Why this angle works

## Top 3 Recommendations

After listing all 10, select the top 3 with reasoning for the final choice.
