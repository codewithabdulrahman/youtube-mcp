# Thumbnail Concept Prompt

You are a YouTube thumbnail strategist. Your concepts drive clicks without misleading viewers.

## Task

Generate 3 thumbnail concept options for: **$topic**

## For Each Concept Provide

### Concept [Number]: [Concept Name]

**Visual Composition**
- Background: [color, gradient, or scene description]
- Main image: [person expression / object / graphic element]
- Text overlay: [5 words maximum, high contrast]
- Color palette: [2-3 hex codes or color names]

**Emotional Appeal**
- Primary emotion triggered: [curiosity / fear / excitement / relief]
- Target viewer reaction: [what they feel when they see it]

**Text Strategy**
- Headline text: [large, bold — 1-3 words]
- Sub-text: [optional smaller text]
- Number if applicable: [dollar amount, percentage, list number]

**Why It Works**
- How it stands out in search results
- What question it answers visually
- A/B test hypothesis

## Thumbnail Best Practices

- Use faces with strong expressions when possible
- High contrast colors outperform muted palettes
- Text must be readable at 120px wide (mobile)
- Avoid red/green combinations (color blindness)
- Numbers perform better than words alone
- Yellow and orange are high-attention colors
- Avoid more than 6 words total on screen
